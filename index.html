About WestJet Airlines

WestJet Airlines is a virtual GeoFS Canadian airline (Not affiliated with the real-life WestJet Airlines Ltd.) founded in 2025, offering domestic and international flights with a modern fleet including Boeing 737s, 787 Dreamliners, and more. Our mission is to provide safe, comfortable, and reliable air travel while connecting passengers to hundreds of destinations around the globe.
