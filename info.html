About WestJet Virtual

WestJet Airlines (GeoFS) is a community-based virtual airline inspired by the real-world WestJet. We operate domestic, international, and long-haul routes across North America, Europe, and Asia with a modern fleet of Boeing aircraft.

Our Mission

To bring realism, professionalism, and community engagement to the GeoFS flight simulator while still allowing freedom. We aim to replicate real airline operations while keeping things fun and accessible for everyone.

WestJet Group & Subsidiaries

WestJet Group has three direct subsidiaries:

  • WestJet Airlines Ltd.
  • WestJet Encore
  • WestJet Cargo

These three groups were decided as useful to ensure the organization of WestJet. Their purpose is to make sure that operations remain structured and specialized for each branch of service.

WestJet Airlines

WestJet Airlines is the main group of WestJet, handling most charter and international flights. It represents the core of WestJet’s operations and is why WestJet is recognized as an international airline. With a modern fleet of 737s, 787s, and 777s, WestJet Airlines connects Canada to the rest of the world.

WestJet Encore

WestJet Encore is the regional branch of WestJet. It operates smaller aircraft such as the Dash 8 Q400 to connect smaller communities with major hubs. This allows passengers from rural or remote areas to easily connect to larger destinations served by WestJet Airlines.

WestJet Cargo

WestJet Cargo is the freight division of WestJet, responsible for transporting goods worldwide. The fleet consists of four Boeing 737-800BCF aircraft designed for fast and efficient logistics. Whether it's urgent shipments or regular cargo operations, WestJet Cargo ensures rapid and reliable service.
