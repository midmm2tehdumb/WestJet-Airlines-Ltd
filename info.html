WestJet Airlines Ltd.

WestJet Airlines is the main group of WestJet, doing most of the charter flights and all international flights. This is why WestJet is considered an international airline.

×

WestJet Encore

WestJet Encore is the regional branch of WestJet. They fly smaller aircraft (Dash 8-400) to bring people to larger airports so that they can traverse further, even if they live in a more rural area.

×

WestJet Cargo

WestJet Cargo is the freighter part of WestJet. They have 4 B737-800BCFs to transport goods across the world quickly and reliably.
