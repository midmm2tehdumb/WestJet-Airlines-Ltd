About WestJet Virtual

WestJet Airlines (GeoFS) is a community-based virtual airline inspired by the real-world WestJet. We operate domestic, international, and long-haul routes across North America, Europe, and Asia with a modern fleet of Boeing aircraft.

Our Mission

To bring realism, professionalism, and community engagement to the GeoFS flight simulator while still allowing freedom. We aim to replicate real airline operations while keeping things fun and accessible for everyone.

WestJet Airlines Ltd.

WestJet Airlines is the main group of WestJet, doing most of the charter flights and all international flights. This is why WestJet is considered an international airline.

×

WestJet Encore

WestJet Encore is the regional branch of WestJet. They fly smaller aircraft (Dash 8-400) to bring people to larger airports so that they can traverse further, even if they live in a more rural area.

×

WestJet Cargo

WestJet Cargo is the freighter part of WestJet. They have 4 B787-800BCFs to transport goods across the world quickly and reliably.
