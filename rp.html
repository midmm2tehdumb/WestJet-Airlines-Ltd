Catering Services

Economy Class Catering (Short & Medium Haul)

Lufthansa's catering for shorter flights is based on the 'Buy on Board' (Retail) concept, 'Onboard Delights', where a variety of high-quality food and drinks are available for purchase.

  • Complimentary Offering: All passengers receive a complimentary bottle of water on all short- and medium-haul flights.
  • Onboard Delights: Passengers can purchase fresh snacks, sandwiches, salads, warm meals, and a variety of beverages (alcoholic and non-alcoholic) from the menu. The selection is regionally inspired.
  • Specific Routes: Free complimentary catering (small snack and beverage) is still provided on select routes, such as those to/from certain international destinations or longer European routes.

Note: The exact offerings depend on flight time and destination, but the core service is Buy-on-Board, plus complimentary water.

Economy Class Catering (Long Haul)

On long-haul international flights, a comprehensive meal service is included in the fare.

  • Main Meal Service: Depending on the flight time, passengers receive at least one full hot meal (with a choice of main courses) and a range of complimentary beverages, including water, soft drinks, wine, and beer.
  • Second Meal/Snack: A second lighter meal, such as a breakfast, a sandwich, or a hot snack, is served before landing on longer routes.
  • Beverage Service: Regular water and drink rounds are provided throughout the flight.

Premium Economy Catering

Premium Economy guests enjoy an enhanced service, which is particularly noticeable on long-haul flights.

  • Enhanced Meal Service: Meals are presented on china tableware (not plastic). Passengers receive a higher quality main course selection compared to Economy Class.
  • Welcome Drink: A non-alcoholic welcome drink is typically offered upon boarding.
  • Beverages: A wide selection of complimentary alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages is included throughout the flight.
  • Additional Snack/Amenity: A bottle of water is provided at the seat, and a small snack or amenity is often offered before or after the main meal service.

Business Class

Experience Lufthansa’s refined service with gourmet menu selections inspired by international cuisine, paired with fine wines and premium beverages.

  • Short- and Medium-Haul: Enhanced service with a full meal served on china, often tailored to the time of day (e.g., hot breakfast, warm lunch/dinner), plus a selection of international wines, soft drinks, and a complimentary bottle of water.
  • Long-Haul Flights (Multi-Course Dining): Multi-course meals crafted by top chefs, including an appetizer, choice of gourmet main courses, cheese course, and dessert. An extensive wine, champagne, and beverage selection is available.
  • Comfort & Amenities: Spacious seats with ample legroom, fully reclining for restful sleep on long-haul flights, plus Lufthansa amenity kits including pillows, blankets, and premium toiletries.
  • 'Restaurant Service' on Long-Haul: Meals are served individually from the galley, mimicking a restaurant experience.

First Class

Indulge in Lufthansa Allegris First Class, where luxury meets personalized service at every step of your journey. This includes the exclusive ground services and private cabin experience.

  • Gourmet Dining: Gourmet à la carte dining with seasonal, exclusive menus and premium wine/champagne pairings, available anytime on demand. The service is fully personalized and flexible.
  • Caviar Service: A signature service on long-haul flights, often offered as an appetizer.
  • Exclusive Ground Services: Access to Lufthansa First Class lounges, the First Class Terminal (in Frankfurt), private check-in, and dedicated boarding via limousine/private car service (in Frankfurt/Munich) for a seamless experience.
  • Comfort & Sleep: Recline fully in spacious suites or lie-flat seats, with plush bedding, premium amenity kits, noise-cancelling headphones, and personalized service from your dedicated cabin crew.
